ABOUT SCHOOL

"Army Public School Ambala Cantt Is Governed By Army Welfare Education Society (AWES), A Registered Body Under The Societies Registration Act XXI Of 1860 For The Purpose Of Establishing And Running Various Army Schools. The Society Has A Chain Of 137 Army Schools/Army Public Schools In India.The school was established in 1981. Army Public School is a Co-ed school affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). It is managed by Army Welfare Education Society."
